Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Play

This game is a great way to begin to improve your memory and recall skills. With any skill, you need to practice to improve those skills. Playing this game, by yourself or with a friend, is a fun way to exercise your brain, improve your ability to memorize and recall information.
